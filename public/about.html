// ORIGIN STORY

I’m a founder and full-stack engineer with a strong eye for design and a passion for architecting scalable, real-world processes from idea to production. I currently serve as CTO at ServiceAgent, where I lead both engineering and product development from zero to one for clients across a wide range of industries.

Before I started building, I moved to the United States from Myanmar at fourteen. This shaped how I approach both life and work. It taught me to adapt quickly, embrace failure, stay curious, and most importantly, learn to enjoy the chaos. These are principles I rely on everyday both in my startup and in how I show up personally.

My work sits at the intersection of product, engineering, AI, and design, with a deep focus on putting the client and end user first. I care about building tools that are not only technically strong, but also meaningful products that genuinely make a difference.

I see life and product-building the same way: a continuous cycle of experimentation, failure, and growth. Whether I’m on a call with a client or deep in building, I aim to show up as the best version of myself: focused, adaptable, and always learning.
